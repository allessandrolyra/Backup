# Define Enterprise Standards

Assess enterprise cloud maturity and define the principles, standards, and roadmap needed to improve architectural consistency over time.

## Focus Areas

- cloud maturity and operating model
- architecture principles
- reusable standards and blueprints
- governance and platform boundaries
- 3 to 12 month roadmap
- organizational dependencies and adoption risk

## Process

1. Assess the current architecture and platform maturity
2. Identify the highest-value standards and principles to formalize
3. Propose a practical roadmap with phases, outcomes, and sequencing
4. Recommend which blueprints or golden templates should exist
5. Highlight change-management risks and likely blockers

## Output

Return:
- maturity assessment
- recommended enterprise principles
- blueprint and standard opportunities
- phased roadmap
- adoption risks and suggested owners
