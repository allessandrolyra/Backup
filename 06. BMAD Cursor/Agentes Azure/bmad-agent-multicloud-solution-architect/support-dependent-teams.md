# Support Dependent Teams

Translate the architecture into actionable guidance for the teams that must build, secure, operate, test, and observe it.

## Target Teams

- SRE
- DevOps
- QA
- Security
- Data and Analytics

## Process

1. Identify which teams are in scope
2. Explain what each team needs from the architecture
3. Surface dependencies, handoffs, and ownership boundaries
4. Call out missing enablers, risks, or ambiguities
5. Suggest how architecture artifacts should be packaged for those teams

## Output

Return:
- team-by-team impact summary
- dependencies and required inputs
- operational and governance implications
- recommended artifacts or follow-up reviews
